Deep lacquer crimson-to-near-black atmospheric background inside the frame with very faint watermark auspicious cloud (xiangyun) texture — clean and non-distracting. Effect: 3D embossed relief with depth, primarily warm gold color with soft luminous warm halo glow. Position: text baseline slightly overflows below the frame's lower edge; text width slightly wider than frame left and right edges by approximately 3% on each side.

**Canvas:** 16:9 horizontal aspect ratio. Canvas fill color #666666 (mid gray). Image should show full icon composition against this gray canvas background.

**Color Palette:** Maximum 3 core hues — warm gold, festive crimson-red, near-black deep tone. All primary colors at high saturation (≥65%). Festive, auspicious, vibrant — not dark, muted, or desaturated.
